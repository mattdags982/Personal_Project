Quality You Can Count On From People You Know

 

Family Farmers: Locust Avenue Farm is a family enterprise, owned by Steven and Diane D’Agostino. Our flower nursery has operated in Bohemia for 38 years. We specialize in growing many varieties of annuals and perennials. We also grow a plentiful selection of vegetables and herbs

 

Quality: Our growers are the most quality-conscious people that ever grew a plant! We grow flowers in greenhouses located on several acres. Every Spring, our quiet nursery transforms into a festival of flowers! Thousands of gorgeous hanging baskets lure our customers back every year. Many come in just to see the beautiful display of color and to welcome back Spring!

 

Bohemia Family Farm 

Established in 1984, Locust Avenue Farm Inc. is one of the largest retail growers in Suffolk County. We grow everything at our location in Bohemia, NY.

Celebrating our 38th season in business for 2022, we are a premier grower of hanging baskets, bedding plants, and combination pots, bushels and urns. We also grow many varieties of perennials and a plentiful selection of vegetables and herbs. Since we grow everything at our farm our prices are very competitive.

If you are a first-time gardener, we have a very helpful staff. We grow an enormous quantity of flowers. Come in and see for yourself. You will be very happy with the beautiful selection!
